Safety & Trust

Open the Tezzo app or website, enter your pickup and drop locations, choose a date, and browse matching rides. Select a ride, choose your seat, and pay securely in-app to confirm your booking instantly.

Yes. Go to "My Rides" and select the booking you want to cancel. Cancellations made more than 2 hours before departure get a full refund. Late cancellations may incur a small fee and can affect your trust score.

All drivers complete Aadhaar-based government ID verification along with a real-time selfie liveness check before they can publish a ride. This helps prevent impersonation and keeps the community safe.

Tezzo supports UPI, debit/credit cards, and in-app wallet payments through our secure payment partner, Razorpay. All transactions are encrypted end-to-end.

Eligible refunds (cancellations made in time, or driver no-shows) are processed back to your original payment method within 5-7 business days.

Ghost Mode lets you ride with extra privacy — your profile, exact pickup point, and chat identity stay hidden from other users, while all safety features (SOS, ID verification, trust score) remain fully active. Available on Explorer and Fleet plans. Learn more →

Tap the SOS button in the app at any time during a ride to alert our 24/7 Trust & Safety team and share your live location with emergency contacts. If you're in immediate danger, also contact local emergency services.
