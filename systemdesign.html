How a Tezzo ride works

From search to destination in 6 seamless steps.

1

Publish or Search

Drivers publish their route, date, seats and suggested price. Passengers search in real time and see matching rides instantly, ranked by relevance.

2

Smart Matching

Our algorithm ranks rides by proximity, ratings, departure time and price — returning results in under 80ms across 200+ cities.

3

Book & Pay Securely

Passengers pay via UPI, card or wallet — fully in-app. Funds are held in escrow and only released after the ride is completed.

4

Live Ride Tracking

Real-time GPS tracking, ETA updates, and route monitoring — all inside the app. Share your live location with up to 5 trusted contacts.

5

Rate & Review

After every ride, both driver and passenger rate each other. Low-rated users are automatically flagged and reviewed by our trust team within 24 hours.

6

Instant Payout

Drivers receive payment instantly via UPI once the ride completes. Tezzo Points are credited for both parties — redeemable on future trips.

Safety & security by design

Every layer of Tezzo is built with your safety in mind — not bolted on after.

🆔

Aadhaar KYC & Liveness

All drivers undergo Aadhaar-based government ID verification plus a real-time selfie liveness check to prevent impersonation fraud.

📍

Live GPS with Auto-SOS

Every ride is tracked in real time. The app automatically triggers an SOS alert if a ride deviates significantly from the planned route.

🔒

Encrypted Escrow Payments

End-to-end encrypted transactions. Passenger funds are held in escrow — the driver only gets paid after the trip is confirmed complete.

🤖

ML-powered Trust Score

Every user has a live trust score calculated from reviews, cancellation rate, ID status, and ride behaviour. Low scores trigger automatic review.

📞

Number Masking

Phone numbers are always masked in-app. Calls and messages go through Tezzo's relay — your real number is never shared with other users.

🧑‍⚖️

24/7 Trust Team

A dedicated team monitors flagged rides, reviews complaints, and can remotely assist any ride in real time — around the clock, every day.

Infrastructure that never sleeps.

Multi-region failover. Auto-scaling. Real-time monitoring. Tezzo is built to be always on.
