Ghost Mode.

Full privacy control without compromising your safety score or community access.

Complete privacy. Full control.

Ghost Mode gives you granular visibility settings — toggle on or off per ride.

👤

Anonymous Profile

Your real name and photo are hidden from other riders. Only Tezzo's trust team can access your verified identity — never other users.

📍

Fuzzy Pickup Location

Your exact pickup point stays private until both sides confirm. Only a ~1km radius is visible to the driver before booking is locked.

💬

E2E Encrypted Messages

All in-app chat is end-to-end encrypted. No phone numbers are ever exchanged. Messages auto-delete 48h after trip completion.

🔔

Invisible Browsing

Browse and view ride listings without drivers seeing you've checked their trip. Your search history is never visible to anyone but you.

⭐

Masked Review Identity

Ratings you leave show only your first name and total trip count. Your full profile link is never attached to a Ghost Mode review.

🛡️

Safety Always On

Ghost Mode is privacy-first, never safety-last. SOS, emergency contacts, ID verification and trust score — all still active on every Ghost ride.

Privacy that works the way you do.

Toggle per ride — Ghost Mode is per-trip, not account-wide. Turn it on or off for each individual journey depending on your comfort.

No impact on ride access — You still see all available rides. Drivers still see your booking request — just anonymised.

Trust score protected — Your rating accumulates privately. A 5-star Ghost still gets priority access to popular seats.

SOS always available — One tap emergency contact sharing still works in full Ghost Mode. Safety is never traded for privacy.
