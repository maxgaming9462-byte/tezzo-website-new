Why we built Tezzo

In 2006, our founder Rahul was a student commuting between Pune and Mumbai every week. Train tickets were scarce, cabs were unaffordable, and buses were unreliable. He started sharing rides with classmates informally — and quickly realised this could be something much bigger.

Today, Tezzo connects 30 million members across India. We're not just a carpooling app — we're building the trust infrastructure for shared travel in a country of 1.4 billion people who deserve better options.

Every trip on Tezzo saves an average of 4.4 kg of CO₂. Across our community, that adds up to over 130,000 tonnes of emissions prevented every year — and growing.

Our journey

Twenty years of making India move smarter.

06
2006

The first shared ride

Rahul shares his car with 3 classmates on the Pune–Mumbai expressway. The idea for Tezzo is born during that 3-hour journey.

10
2010

Tezzo is officially founded

Incorporated in Pune. First 1,000 members join within 90 days — word-of-mouth only, from Pune and Mumbai college networks.

14
2014

Mobile app launches

iOS and Android apps launch simultaneously. Bookings increase 10× in 6 months. Expansion to Delhi NCR and Bengaluru begins.

17

Series B — ₹280 Cr raised

Tezzo expands to 40+ cities nationwide. In-app UPI payments launch with Razorpay. Trust score system introduced.
